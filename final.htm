Work and Hobbies

I have worked at Burger King since I was 14, almost 15. I have been working there for nearly 6 years now. Over the years, I have learned one thing, that is that the customer is most definitely not always right. I never realized how nasty some people got when it came to fast food. Of course, most customers are nice and understanding. I have gotten to know customers by name and memorize their orders, which can come in handy, especially when there's a lunch or dinner rush.

I really enjoy having her as a teacher, she is a really sweet old lady. I also like to do little projects like diamond paintings and puzzles.
